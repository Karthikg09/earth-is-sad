Earth is sad

Earth is sad' is a collection of 5040 randomly generated and stylistically curated NFTs that exist on the Polygon Blockchain, inspired by Earth and the climate crisis.

Our environment plays an important role in healthy living and the existence of life on planet earth. Earth is a home for different living species and we all are dependent on the environment for food, air, water, and other needs. Therefore, it is important for every individual to save and protect our environment.

A minimum of 10% of the net revenue of both the primary and secondary market will go to charities that take a global and holistic approach to improving the understanding of all issues related to the conservation of and developing and scaling technologies to rid the oceans of plastic and converse the environment.
